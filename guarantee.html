📋 Guarantee Terms
🛡️ Our On-Time Guarantee

At Rollo Heating & Plumbing, we respect your time. If our engineer does not arrive within the agreed 2-hour arrival window for a scheduled appointment, you may be entitled to compensation of up to €100, subject to the terms below.

✅ What qualifies
  • Your appointment was pre-booked (not an emergency call-out)
  • You confirmed the appointment via our SMS confirmation link
  • You were present and accessible at the property during the arrival window
  • The engineer did not arrive within the agreed 2-hour window
  • No notification of delay was received from us before the window closed
  • Your claim is submitted within 48 hours of the missed appointment
✕ Exclusions — guarantee does not apply if:
  • Met Éireann issued a Status Orange or Red weather warning for your county on the day
  • A road closure, major traffic incident, or emergency works blocked access (AA Roadwatch logged)
  • You were not home or accessible when the engineer arrived or attempted to contact you
  • You provided an incorrect address, access code, or entry instructions
  • You cancelled or rescheduled within 24 hours of your appointment
  • A previous job on the engineer's schedule overran by 90 minutes or more, and you received an SMS notification of the delay
  • Access to the property was blocked by circumstances outside our control (utilities, parking restrictions, etc.)
  • The appointment was an emergency or same-day call-out (fixed times not guaranteed)
  • The claim relates to a follow-up visit rather than the initial appointment
  • You have submitted a claim for the same property within the previous 12 months
💰 Compensation
  • Maximum compensation: €100 per incident
  • Issued as a credit note against future work, or refund of deposit paid
  • Cannot be combined with any other discount or offer
  • One claim per customer per 12-month period
  • Rollo Heating & Plumbing reserves the right to investigate and approve or reject any claim
  • Our decision is final
📝 How to Claim
  • Reply CLAIM to any of our SMS messages within 48 hours
  • Or call our office on 01-XXX-XXXX and quote your job reference number
  • We will review your claim within 2 working days
  • Approved claims processed within 5 working days
⚠️ LEGAL NOTE
These are draft terms for review purposes only. Before publishing this guarantee, have your solicitor review the final version. Ensure your public liability insurance covers compensation payments and that your T&Cs are registered where required under Irish consumer law.
💡 Tips for staying protected
✅ Always get SMS confirmation
A customer who replies YES has confirmed they'll be home. If they don't reply, the guarantee doesn't activate.
📱 Engineer checks in via app
GPS check-in timestamps prove the engineer arrived. No check-in = potential claim. Always check in.
⏰ Notify early if running late
Sending a "running late" SMS before the window closes cancels any claim — and customers appreciate honesty.
🚫 Emergency jobs are excluded
Same-day call-outs don't carry the guarantee. Make sure your booking form is clear about job type.
📊 Track your claims rate
Target: 0 claims per month. If claims appear, it's a scheduling or routing problem — fix the root cause.
