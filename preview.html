Engineer Mobile App

A fully functional ServiceM8-style job management app for field engineers — works on any phone, offline.

How it works
Tap any feature card to navigate the app — or click directly inside the phone screen.
🗂
Jobs List
Today's jobs with coloured status bars, customer name, address, time. Tap any job to open the full job card.
🔍
Search & Filter
Search by customer, address or job type. Filter by Today / Upcoming / Queue / All — instant results.
💬
Team Inbox
Messages from the whole team. Rollo AI sends job reminders, schedule changes, and invoice alerts.
⚔️
Rollo AI Chat
Ask Rollo anything — reschedule a job, generate an invoice, get a customer's details. Instant replies.
📊
My Profile & Stats
Your jobs today, total jobs completed, links to the desktop dashboard and Ops Hub.
💡 Tip — Tap any job card
Inside the job detail view you'll find Check In/Out, status picker, Notes diary, Photos, Materials, and one-tap invoicing.
